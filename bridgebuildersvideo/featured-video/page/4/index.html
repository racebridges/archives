TAMING THE FIRE: A BLACK HISTORY SEARCH

Posted on 01/20/2016 by bridgebuildersvideo

by Storyteller Sheila Arnold

One day an angry black teenage girl – Sheila – stormed into her history class and demanded to know why she had never heard about black inventors. Her favorite teacher, who happened to be white, was faced with a decision.  In making that decision, an entire classroom of students was changed and history was given more relevance.

This is the story of the discovery of African American History. It is about the growing realization of the pain and of the power of knowing and appropriating a heritage that had often been long hidden. Length – 12 minutes
