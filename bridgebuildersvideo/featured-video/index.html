ANOTHER WAY WEST

Posted on 09/21/2020 by bridgebuildersvideo

by Storyteller Jane Stenson

At age 16, in 1855, Jane’s great-grandfather sailed from N.Y. around the Horn to San Francisco where he was stranded! He took a job with Wells Fargo as a treasure agent in the Sacramento-Shasta Mining District. This was the home of the Shasta Indian Nation. Jane Stenson’s family story takes us into the gold rush days of California, the Vigilance Committee and the Pony Express. Missing from Jane’s family story is the sad chapter that deals with the forced movement of the Shasta Native American tribe from their homeland… aided by Jane’s family hero.    Length ~11 minutes
