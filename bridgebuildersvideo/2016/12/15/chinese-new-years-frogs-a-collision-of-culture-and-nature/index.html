CHINESE NEW YEAR’S FROGS: A COLLISION OF CULTURE AND NATURE

Posted on 12/15/2016 by bridgebuildersvideo

by Storyteller Linda Yemoto. One time Park Ranger and Naturalist Linda Yemoto  describes her encounter with a group of well-intentioned Chinese Americans bearing bullfrogs.  This surprising incident illustrates how cultural differences can have unintended consequences and how cultural awareness can lead to greater understanding and a better outcome for all. ~9 minutes
