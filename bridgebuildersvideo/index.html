Welcome to BridgeBuilder Videos

The short videos on this site present professional
storytellers telling original stories. These stories seek to
illuminate some of the inculturality themes of the
SVD 2012 Chapter and to bring alive our Congregational
Directions. The brief questions and reflections seek to
connect the themes of these stories to our SVD life.

These videos have been produced by Bridges Studio
(previously called Angels Studio) of the SVD Chicago
Province for earlier use in media and online for schools
and adults. You will find an archive of over 200 diversity
videos produced by Bridges Studio that can be accessed at
RaceBridgesStudio.com for your interest and free use.

This site is curated by Derek Simons SVD.
